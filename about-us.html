Welcome to Indian Utsava

Indian Utsava is a grocery subscription service designed to make festival shopping hassle-free. By subscribing to our plans, customers pay ₹350, ₹500, or ₹1000 per month for a year and receive a complete grocery package for the Ugadi festival.

We focus on convenience, affordability, and quality, ensuring our customers get the best festive shopping experience without last-minute stress.
