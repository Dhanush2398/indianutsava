About Our Company

A Smarter Way to Shop, Save, and Celebrate

Welcome to Indian Utsava, where we believe in making your shopping experience simpler and more rewarding.
ಇಂಡಿಯನ್ ಉತ್ಸವಕ್ಕೆ ಸ್ವಾಗತ, ಇಲ್ಲಿಗೆಂದು ನಿಮ್ಮ ಶಾಪಿಂಗ್ ಅನುಭವವನ್ನು ಸರಳಗೊಳಿಸಲು ಮತ್ತು ಹೆಚ್ಚು ಲಾಭದಾಯಕವಾಗಿಸಲು ನಾವು ನಂಬಿದ್ದೇವೆ.

Our unique business model allows you to invest a small monthly installment and, once a year, receive groceries worth the total amount you’ve contributed.

At Indian Utsava, we are committed to delivering top-quality groceries that cater to all your household needs.

Indian Utsava: Simplifying Grocery Shopping for You

Our mission is to make grocery shopping a smooth, affordable, and convenient experience while helping our members save both time and money.
